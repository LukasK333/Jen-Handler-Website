Clinical Supervision

I also provide private clinical supervision to therapists looking to deepen their therapeutic skills. I work in a contemporary relational psychoanalytic framework and make use of that framework to help you to explore any themes and issues may be coming up for you in the treatment with your patients.

I am currently a supervisor and the Co-Director of Supervision for the Four-Year Adult Training Program at the National Institute for the Psychotherapies for candidates completing advanced training in psychoanalysis. If you would like to learn more about my approach to supervision or would like to know more about working with me as a private supervisor, please contact me.
