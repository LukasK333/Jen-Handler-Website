Relationship Therapy

I offer individual psychodynamic psychotherapy and psychoanalysis as well as couples/relationship therapy.

Before we start working together, we will schedule an initial consultation. During this process (over one or more 60-minute sessions), we will talk about your treatment goals and give us a chance to learn more about each other.

For individual sessions, my Initial consultation fee is $300. My ongoing treatment fee is $250 for a 45-monute session. For couples, my consultation fee and ongoing treatment fee is $300 for a 60-minute session. In certain instances, a sliding scale fee may be available.

I am happy to provide all necessary documentation for out of network reimbursement from your health insurance.

If you would like to learn more about how I work or to set up a brief 10-minute consultation, please feel free to contact me.
