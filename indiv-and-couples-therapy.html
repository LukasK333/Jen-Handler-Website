Individual and Couples Therapy

I work with individuals and couples who are navigating relationship challenges, life transitions, and the pressures of demanding professional environments.

My approach is psychodynamic and relational, with a focus on helping people better understand themselves, their patterns, and their relationships. I aim to create a space that feels thoughtful, engaged, and collaborative.

We begin with an initial consultation, where we’ll talk about what’s bringing you in and what you’re hoping for. This is also a chance for you to get a sense of how I work and whether it feels like a good fit.

Fees and Insurance
Individual sessions: $250 (45 minutes)
Initial consultation: $300
Couples sessions: $300 (60 minutes)

I am in-network with Cigna and NYU student health insurance (Wellfleet). A sliding scale is available.

I am happy to provide all necessary documentation for out of network reimbursement from your health insurance.

If you would like to learn more about how I work or to set up a free 15-minute consultation, please feel free to contact me.
